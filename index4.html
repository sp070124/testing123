This is Anfield

Retail Store

With all the latest kits, LFC Fashion and souvenirs bring home a memento of your trip to Anfield from the LFC Retail Store. The store is located on Walton Breck Road.

Fan Zones

There are two Fan Zones, one on Anfield Road where you can find food, drink and live music and a Fan Zone at Paisley Square which is home to live entertainment, food & soft drinks, retail offerings.

Stadium Tour

Tours of our historic Stadium are available throughout the year, both on a matchday and on a non-matchday.
